Who We Are

A voice for Nebraska families affected by rare diseases

Our Mission

Nebraska RARE is dedicated to empowering families impacted by rare diseases through education, advocacy, and community inclusion. We strive to elevate awareness, partner with healthcare providers, and communities to ensure every person with a rare condition is understood, supported, and valued. Together, we build a compassionate network where all families feel seen, heard, and never alone.

Who We Serve

Nebraska RARE supports individuals and families across our state who are navigating life with rare diseases:

  • Children and adults living with rare conditions
  • Parents and caregivers seeking resources and support
  • Families in rural communities with limited access to specialized care
  • Healthcare providers seeking to better serve rare disease patients
  • Educators and community members working to foster inclusion
