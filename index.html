Our Mission

What drives everything we do

Nebraska RARE is dedicated to empowering families impacted by rare diseases through education, advocacy, and community inclusion. We strive to elevate awareness and partner with healthcare providers and communities to ensure every child and adult with a rare condition is understood, supported, and valued.

Together, we build a compassionate network where all families feel seen, heard, and never alone.
