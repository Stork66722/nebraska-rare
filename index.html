Our Mission

Nebraska RARE is dedicated to empowering families impacted by rare diseases through education, advocacy, and community inclusion. We strive to elevate awareness, partner with healthcare providers, and communities to ensure every person with a rare condition is understood, supported, and valued. Together, we build a compassionate network where all families feel seen, heard, and never alone.
